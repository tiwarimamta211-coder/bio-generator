# AI Bio Generator – Use Karne Ka Tarika (Hindi Guide)

**Yeh tool kya karta hai:** Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, YouTube, Facebook aur WhatsApp ke liye AI se professional bio banata hai — 4 variations ek saath, copy-paste ready.

---

## Step-by-Step Guide

**Step 1 — Platform Choose Karein**
Sabse pehle top mein 6 platform cards dikhenge (Instagram 📸, Twitter/X 🐦, LinkedIn 💼, YouTube ▶️, Facebook 👍, WhatsApp 💬). Jis platform ke liye bio chahiye uska card click karein. Har platform ka character limit alag-alag hai aur wo automatically apply ho jayega (jaise Instagram = 150 chars, WhatsApp = 139 chars).

**Step 2 — Naam/Brand Daalein**
"Naam / Brand Name" box mein apna naam ya business ka naam type karein. Jaise — "Mamta Sharma" ya "IncomeAIHub".

**Step 3 — Profession/Niche Likhein**
Apna kaam ya niche batayein — jaise "Freelance Designer", "Fitness Coach", "Content Creator", "Stock Market Trader". Yeh jitna clear hoga, bio utna accurate banega.

**Step 4 — Keywords Daalein**
Comma se separate karke apne interests/keywords likhein. Jaise: `travel, coffee lover, dog mom, startup founder`. Ye AI ko bio ka "flavor" decide karne mein help karta hai.

**Step 5 — Call To Action (Optional)**
Agar bio mein koi action line chahiye to yahan likhein — jaise "DM for collabs", "Link below 👇", "Book a call". Agar nahi chahiye to khali chhod dein.

**Step 6 — Tone Select Karein**
Niche 6 chips dikhenge: Professional, Funny, Bold, Minimal, Aesthetic, Motivational. Jo tone aapke bio mein chahiye usko click karein (sirf ek select hoga).

**Step 7 — Toggles Adjust Karein**
- 😊 **Emojis** — bio mein emoji chahiye ya nahi
- #️⃣ **Hashtags** — Instagram/Twitter ke liye hashtags on rakhein, LinkedIn ke liye off
- 🇮🇳 **Hinglish Style** — agar Hindi-English mix wala casual bio chahiye to ON karein

**Step 8 — Generate Button Dabayein**
"⚡ GENERATE BIO" button click karein. Kuch second mein AI 4 different bio options bana dega, har ek ka character count bhi saath mein dikhega (green = limit ke andar, red = limit cross).

**Step 9 — Copy Karein**
Jo bio sabse pasand aaye, uske saamne "📋 COPY" button dabayein. Bio clipboard mein copy ho jayega — seedha apni profile mein paste kar dein.

---

## Zaruri Baatein

⚠️ Agar koi bio character limit se zyada lamba dikhe (red warning), to use use na karein — keywords kam karke dobara generate karein.

⚠️ Hashtags sirf Instagram/Twitter ke liye useful hain — LinkedIn professional profile ke liye hashtags avoid karna better hai.

⚠️ Tool ko kaam karne ke liye internet connection zaruri hai, kyunki ye AI (Claude) ko real-time call karta hai.

✅ Generated bio sirf ek suggestion hai — aap apne style ke according usme thoda edit bhi kar sakte hain.
