📘 AI Bio Generator – Hindi Mein Pura Guide

Yeh tool aapke liye Instagram, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, YouTube, Facebook aur WhatsApp ke liye professional aur attractive bio AI ki madad se generate karta hai — bilkul free mein.

🔹 Tool Kaise Use Karein

  1. Platform select karein — jis social media ke liye bio chahiye uska card click karein. Har platform ka character limit alag hota hai, jo automatically apply ho jata hai.
  2. Naam ya brand name daalein.
  3. Profession ya niche likhein — jaise "Fitness Coach", "Freelance Writer", "Travel Blogger" etc.
  4. Keywords/interests comma se separate karke daalein — jitna specific, bio utna personalized banega.
  5. Call To Action (optional) — agar aap chahte hain bio mein koi action line ho, jaise "DM for collabs" ya "Link below".
  6. Tone select karein — Professional, Funny, Bold, Minimal, Aesthetic ya Motivational.
  7. Toggles adjust karein — Emojis, Hashtags, aur Hinglish style on/off karein apni pasand se.
  8. "⚡ GENERATE BIO" button dabayein — AI 4 unique bio variations banayega, har ek ka character count bhi dikhega.
  9. Jo bio pasand aaye uske saamne "Copy" button dabayein — bio clipboard mein copy ho jayega, seedha paste karein.

🔹 Important Baatein

  • Har bio ke saath character count dikhta hai — green color matlab limit ke andar hai, red matlab limit cross ho gaya.
  • Agar bio limit se zyada lamba ho, to "Regenerate" karein ya keywords kam karein.
  • Hinglish toggle on karne se bio Hindi-English mix style mein banega — jo Indian audience ke liye zyada relatable hota hai.
  • Hashtags sirf un platforms ke liye useful hain jahan hashtags chalte hain (Instagram, Twitter/X) — LinkedIn ke liye usually avoid karein.

🔹 Kis Tarah Ke Log Use Kar Sakte Hain

  • Content creators jo apna Instagram/YouTube bio improve karna chahte hain
  • Freelancers aur business owners jo LinkedIn profile professional banana chahte hain
  • Students jo apna personal brand build kar rahe hain
  • Anyone jo quickly multiple bio options try karna chahta hai
